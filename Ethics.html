Spy Drones: Big Brother Analysis

Pros: The pros of having spy drones patrol our local streets is a reduction in crime. Having drones that can detect crime in the community in would benefit police in monitoring crime. Our streets would be safe and the 'bad guys' would have been caught. Also, they are already used in the military and have been protecting us for years.

Cons: Unfortunately, no matter how cool and awesome these things may look, they bring a lot of ethical concerns. One for one, the issue of privacy. If one wants to argue that, they will only monitor people that have committed crimes in the past or are suspicious of, what is stopping them on spying on random civilians. Also, there the issue of the innate biases that could be programmed into these bots that will have to make inferences and decisions about what is a crime and who they will target. This could be very dangerous.
