About Us

Mission Statement: To reduce the amount of crime in the city by monitoring activity within the area.

Intended Users: Local Government

Motivation: To keep the cities safer and make citizens safer

The Big Brother Drone was a vision of Team Sophia. We, Team Sophia, intend to inform the audience about the use of new security spy drones. They will be introduced in the near future and their main purpose serves to reduce the amount of crime in the city by monitoring activity within the area. These drones will be called Big Brother and will provide a safety net to nearly all of the population. By introducing these through local government rather than federal government, they will be seen as more trustworthy and reliable. Anyone who wishes for their life, liberty, and property to be within safekeeping should get on board with these new drones, and use this website to stay informed about them.

IS THIS EVEN ETHICAL?

The ethics of these spy Drone has remained a controversial topic of discussion. Many people find the idea of spy drones as an invasion of privacy and a way for the government to limit our right to it. These people claim that the government should trust its people to make the right decisions. Others feel that some people that have broken laws in the pass or who are potentially dangerous to society deserve to have limited rights. Who is correct?
